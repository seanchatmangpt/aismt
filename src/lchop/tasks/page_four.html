What is Privacy Compliance?

Privacy compliance refers to the process of ensuring that an organization is following all laws, regulations, and guidelines related to the collection, use, and protection of personal information. This includes data privacy laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Privacy compliance is important for both businesses and individuals. For businesses, it helps to build trust with customers and avoid costly fines and legal consequences. For individuals, it ensures that their personal information is being handled responsibly and ethically.
